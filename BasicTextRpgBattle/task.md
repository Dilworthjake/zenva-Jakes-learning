# RPG Mini Project

## The premise

- health set for each player
- each character can select a move
  - attack or heal
- adjust other players health to correspond with attack
- adjust own health to correspond with heal
- game ends when either character health hits 0

### moves

- normal attack to do standard damage
- special attack for a minor heal and still deal small damage
- heal for a major heal with no damage
- reckless attack for damage to self and major damage to enemy
